The Joe Rogan Experience

About

The Joe Rogan Experience is a podcast hosted by Joe Rogan, a stand-up comedian, actor, and martial artist. The podcast began in 2009 and has since become one of the most popular and influential podcasts in the world. The show features Rogan engaging in long-form conversations with guests from a wide range of fields, including science, technology, philosophy, politics, entertainment, and more. Guests have included Elon Musk, Neil deGrasse Tyson, Jordan Peterson, and Bernie Sanders, among many others.

History

  • The Joe Rogan Experience began in December 2009.
  • The first guest on the show was Brian Redban.
  • The show quickly gained popularity and has since become one of the most downloaded podcasts on iTunes.
  • In 2015, Rogan signed an exclusive deal with podcast network, PodcastOne, to host his show.
  • In 2020, Rogan signed a multi-year licensing deal with Spotify, making the Joe Rogan Experience available exclusively on Spotify.
